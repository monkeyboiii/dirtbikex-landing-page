This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar technologies DirtBikeX uses, why we use them, and the choices you have. It supplements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your actions and preferences over time (sign-in state, language, etc.). "Similar technologies" include local storage, session storage, and mobile-app identifiers — they serve the same purpose without being technically called cookies.

## How DirtBikeX uses cookies

We use the **minimum** cookies needed to make the Services work, plus privacy-friendly analytics that do not track you across other sites. We do **not** use third-party advertising cookies, behavioral profiling, or cross-site tracking.

### 1. Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the Services to function. Without them, you cannot sign in or use core features. We do not need your consent for these (EU ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3) exemption).

| Cookie / token | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| Session cookie | Keeps you signed in to the forum | Session (clears when you close the browser) or up to 90 days if "remember me" is checked |
| CSRF token | Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks on forms | Session |
| Sign-in flow cookie | Manages the OIDC sign-in handshake with our identity provider | A few minutes during sign-in |
| Language preference | Remembers your chosen language (e.g., `en`, `zh`) | 1 year |
| Theme preference | Remembers light/dark mode if you set it | 1 year |

### 2. Analytics (privacy-friendly, cookieless)

On the marketing website (dirtbikex.com), we use **privacy-friendly analytics that do not set cookies, do not assign persistent identifiers, and do not track you across other sites.** Aggregated metrics like page views, country (derived from IP, then discarded), referrer, and device type help us understand traffic and improve the site. No consent banner is required for cookieless analytics in the European Union under current regulatory guidance.

### 3. Forum (Discourse) cookies

The forum at forum.dirtbikex.com may set additional cookies that are part of the underlying forum software, including:

| Cookie | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| `_t` | Authentication token | Up to 1 year |
| `_forum_session` | Forum session | Session |
| `_bypass_cache` | Performance fix in some flows | Short-lived |
| Notification-state cookies | Tracks which notifications you've seen so we don't repeat them | Variable |

All of these are first-party and strictly necessary or functional.

### 4. Mobile app identifiers

The iOS app does not use traditional cookies (cookies are a web technology). Instead, it uses:

- a **push-notification token** from Apple to send you push notifications, if you have opted in;
- **device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting** provided by a major mobile-platform service — these may include the Apple-provided Identifier for Vendor (IDFV), but **do not include the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)** unless you affirmatively grant App Tracking Transparency permission, which we do not currently request;
- local storage of preferences such as language and view state.

## Cookies we do **not** use

- Advertising cookies / retargeting pixels
- Cross-site tracking
- Third-party social-share buttons that load until you click them
- Fingerprinting
- Sale or sharing of cookie data with data brokers

## Your choices

- **Browser controls.** Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Note: if you block strictly necessary cookies, sign-in won't work.
- **Mobile controls.** On iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → App Tracking Transparency, and Analytics & Improvements.
- **Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control.** We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal as an opt-out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under California and other state laws. Since we do not sell or share, this is a confirmatory signal for us.
- **Withdraw consent.** Where we collected your consent for any non-essential cookie, you can withdraw at any time without affecting prior processing. Email **support@dirtbikex.com**.
