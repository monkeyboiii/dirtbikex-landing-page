This is a placeholder privacy policy for **DirtBikeX**. It will be replaced with a real policy before launch.

## Information we collect

- Account information you provide when registering for the forum (handled by our self-hosted Discourse instance at `forum.dirtbikex.com`).
- Content you post publicly to the forum.
- Standard server logs (IP address, user agent, request paths) for security and operational purposes.

## How we use it

- To provide the forum service.
- To moderate the community and enforce our terms.
- To diagnose technical problems.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data to third parties.
- We don't run ad networks or behavioral tracking on this site.

## Contact

Questions? Reach us via the forum at [forum.dirtbikex.com](https://forum.dirtbikex.com).
