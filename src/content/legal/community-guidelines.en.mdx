DirtBikeX is a place for riders to share trails, builds, wipeouts, technique, gear, and the occasional bad joke. These Guidelines describe how we expect everyone to behave so the community stays useful and welcoming.

The Guidelines apply everywhere on the Services — the forum, the iOS app, direct messages, and any official DirtBikeX space. They are part of our [Terms of Use](/terms). If you violate them, we may remove content, restrict your account, or close it.

---

## Be a good neighbor

- **Be respectful.** Disagree about jets, suspension setup, and bike brands all you want — attack the argument, not the person.
- **Welcome newcomers.** Everyone was a first-time rider once. If a question is basic, answer it kindly or scroll past.
- **Stay on topic.** Use the right category. Off-topic content gets moved or closed.

## Don't post things that make the forum worse

We will remove content that is:

- **Harassment.** Targeted insults, slurs, doxxing, threats, or sustained personal attacks.
- **Hate speech.** Content attacking people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, or serious disease.
- **Sexually explicit.** Pornography, sexual content involving real people without consent, or any sexual content involving minors. Zero tolerance — see "Reporting child sexual exploitation" below.
- **Graphic violence.** Gratuitous gore, real-world violence used for shock, or content glorifying violence against people or animals.
- **Illegal.** Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activity, including drug trafficking, weapons sales, theft, or fraud.
- **Dangerous riding instruction.** Instructions designed to defeat safety equipment, encourage reckless behavior, or coach others into riding situations likely to cause serious injury or death. Sharing your gnarly crash story is fine — telling someone to remove their brakes is not.
- **Privacy violations.** Posting another person's private information (home address, phone number, workplace, government ID, financial information) without their consent.
- **Impersonation.** Pretending to be another person, brand, employee, or moderator. Parody clearly labeled as parody is fine.
- **Spam.** Repeated unsolicited promotion, irrelevant links, mass-posting, or any automated posting.
- **Malware and phishing.** Links to malware, exploit kits, credential-stealing pages, or anything that compromises another user's security.
- **Off-platform threats and brigading.** Coordinating off-DirtBikeX harassment, raiding other communities, or organizing brigades.
- **AI-generated content masquerading as human experience.** Posting AI-generated photos, ride logs, or reviews while claiming they are your own first-hand experience. Clearly labeled AI content (e.g., concept art, summaries) is fine.

## Sponsored content and disclosure

- If a post is sponsored by DirtBikeX, it will be clearly labeled and posted in the **Sponsors** category by a verified sponsor account.
- **If you have a material connection to a product, brand, or service you discuss — disclose it.** This includes free gear, paid trips, affiliate links, sponsorships, or an ownership interest. "I got these tires for free from XYZ Tires" at the top of the post is enough.
- **Affiliate links** are allowed in moderation, with clear disclosure. Excessive affiliate spam will be treated as spam.

## User-to-user transactions

The forum is not a marketplace. We do not host listings, hold payments, verify identity, or escrow goods.

- If you choose to buy from, sell to, or trade with another user, **you do so at your own risk**.
- We **strongly** recommend in-person inspection, established payment-protection services, and trusted escrow.
- Scammers and bad-faith sellers will be removed and reported. If you are scammed, please report the user; we cannot recover funds or recover property, but we can stop them from doing it again.

## Intellectual property

- **Post what you have the right to post.** Don't upload copyrighted images, videos, manuals, or service-manual scans without permission.
- **Credit creators.** When you share someone else's photo or video, link back and name them.
- **Fair use exists** — short quotes, commentary, review, parody, and educational use are generally fine.
- **DMCA notices** can be sent to **support@dirtbikex.com**; see our [Copyright Policy](/dmca).

## Privacy of fellow riders

- Don't share another rider's identity, plate, address, or workplace without their consent.
- If you film at a public event and someone asks you to take down a video of them, please do.
- Don't post screenshots of private messages without the sender's consent, except to report harassment to moderators.

## How we moderate

- **Human moderators review reports.** Automated tools may flag posts (spam filters, image scanning for known illegal content), but human moderators make the final call.
- **Warnings and timeouts** are our first response for most violations. Severe or repeated violations result in account closure.
- **Edits and removals.** We may edit a post to redact personal information, fix obvious formatting, or remove a violating section. We will note that an edit was made.
- **Appeals.** If you think a moderation action was wrong, reply to the moderator's message or email **support@dirtbikex.com**. A different team member will review.

## Reporting

- **Flag the post.** Use the "Flag" or "Report" function on any post or message.
- **Email** **support@dirtbikex.com** for issues that need a human, including harassment, doxxing, or impersonation.
- **For child sexual exploitation:** email **support@dirtbikex.com** immediately. We report all instances to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement as required by U.S. law.
- **For imminent threats of violence or self-harm:** contact local emergency services first. Then notify us so we can preserve evidence.

## Researchers, press, and law enforcement

- **Researchers:** academic, independent, and good-faith security research is welcome. If you need to scrape, please contact us first at **support@dirtbikex.com**.
- **Press:** quoting public forum content with attribution and a link is fine. For interviews or comment, contact **support@dirtbikex.com**.
- **Law enforcement:** valid legal process can be served at **support@dirtbikex.com**.

## We make the final call

These Guidelines cannot list every possible behavior. We reserve the right to take action against anything that, in our reasonable judgment, harms the community or the Services, even if it is not explicitly listed here.

We also reserve the right to **leave content up** that some users find objectionable, when in our judgment it is part of legitimate community discussion (e.g., honest negative reviews, criticism of brands or public figures, debate about controversial techniques).

## Changes

We may update these Guidelines as the community grows. Material changes will be posted with a date and a summary of what changed.
